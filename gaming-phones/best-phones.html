    1 . Red Magic 7 - Best overall

    Red Magic 7

    PRICE : NPR 80,000 - NPR 147,000

    TOP NOTCH STATEMENT :
    Red Magic smartphones offered by Nubia’s has the reputation as having some of the best gaming based displays, and which is just as true with the latest in the field , the Red Magic 7. Red Magic 7 has a fast 6.8in 165Hz OLED panel screen with impressive 720Hz touch rate that beats practically almost every other smartphones in the market. Though we might not notice much difference in real-world performance, it mostly satifies the needs for gamers to have the best gaming experience .
    The smartphone is paired with the flagship specs including Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 with the specs as 12/16/18GB of RAM, 128/256GB of storage and three-fan cooling system running smoothly for during longer gaming sessions. There are some gaming features including side-mounted touch-based shoulder triggers ,an advanced haptic feedback as well the ability to control performance as per the game.




    Red Magic 7 Overview :

    Design and Display

    Nubia has used this smartphone as an opportunity to highlight the Red Magic 7’s key specs by printing them all over the back. The smartphone is full of some of the best specs ,Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 chip, 64Mp camera, 165Hz screen and 720Hz touch sampling rate all on board. Nubia has managed the cameras at the back of the display which means there's almost no movement when kept flat on a table. The smartphone is 9.5mm thick. When Flipped , the phone reveals the same size display , the Bezels are a touch larger than 6S Pro.

    Performance and Memory

    Despite its price starting at mid-range, Nubia has included the latest and greatest Qualcomm chip in Red Magic handsets.The Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 chip is fitted in this model by Nubia. Qualcomm claims that the move to 4nm processor has brought big performance improvements, It also,comes with support for 5G networks.
    The smartphone is provided with strong internet connection, it means gaming on the Red Magic 7 is going to be fun and recreating for sure. The games like call of Duty , pubg and heavy games are generally to run on this device with greater performance support and This smartphone seems to be one of the best gaming smartphone as per its specs as well cpu performance.


    Specifications :

    • CPU : Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 1
    • REAR CAMERA : 64Mp main, 8Mp ultrawide, 2Mp macro cameras
    • RAM : 12/16/18 GB RAM
    • FRONT CAMERA : 8Mp selfie camera
    • SCREEN SIZE : 6.8in Full HD+ (1080×2400) OLED display, 20:9, 165Hz
    • STORAGE : 128/256GB internal storage (non-expandable)

    2 . OnePlus 9 Pro

    OnePlus 9 Pro

    PRICE : Around NPR.119,990

    TOP NOTCH STATEMENT :
    OnePlus has always struggled with releasing flagship series that created a huge buzz in the market. But with the new OnePlus 9 Pro 5G, OnePlus definitely makes a statement, a bold one too. OnePlus partnered with the Swedish camera tech giant Hasselblad, which is famous for its capturing of the moon landing in the NASA Apollo Mission. Brand new sensors on the rear camera (more on that later) and a superfast wireless fast charging are some of the hallmarks that OnePlus has set with its new OnePlus 9 Pro 5G.

    OnePlus 9 Pro Overview :

    Design and Display

    Starting with the display,The OnePlus 9 Pro has a 6.7-inch AMOLED panel display with a HD+ resolution, a curved screen with a circular shaped punch-hole cutout for the selfie camera. Company claims that the curve has been optimized to reduce the touches to provide premium feel of the display.
    The Fluid Display 2.0 supports 120Hz refresh rate and a 240Hz touch sampling rate. As per OnePlus,the display can adjust between 1Hz and 120Hz based on contents on the screen.

    Performance and Memory

    OnePlus is improving its technology to keep the temperature of the device in control level. According to the company, there are five-layered cooling mechanism that can keep the device cooler after 20 minutes of gameplay.
    Games like the PUBG Mobile, COD Mobile, League of Legends are optimized to support the 360Hz touch rate of the phone. OnePlus calls it as the (Hyper Touch).
    The Snapdragon 888 chipset which is the most advanced chipset that Qualcomm has offered right now. It has featured a tri-cluster CPU design with a Cortex X1 “Prime Core” core clocked at 2.84GHz. All in all , there are 3 Cortex A78 (performance cores) and 4 Cortex A55 (efficiency cores).


    Specifications :

    • CPU : Qualcomm SM8350 Snapdragon 888
    • REAR CAMERA : 48 MP (f/1.8)
    • RAM : 8 GB
    • FRONT CAMERA : 16 MP (f/2.0)
    • SCREEN SIZE : 6.7 inches
    • STORAGE : Available in 128 GB

    3 . Xiaomi 12

    Xiaomi 12

    PRICE : NPR.87,999 (8/256)

    Xiaomi 12 Overview :

    Design and Display

    Xiaomi 12 has a 6.28″ AMOLED screen with curved sides. It is an Full HD+ panel having support for 12-bit color depth, 120Hz refresh rate, HDR10+, and Dolby Vision. The display has also been awarded DisplayMate’s A+ certification.
    The device is equipped with 6.28″ AMOLED panel, 120Hz refresh rate, Up to 480Hz touch sampling rate, Dolby Vision, HDR10+, 1100 nits max brightness, 12-bit colors The back of the phone has a glass build, whereas the side frames are made of metal. Similarly, Xiaomi has used Gorilla Glass Victus on the front and currently the phone is available in three color options, plus a Special Edition with Wild Green Leather.
    At the backside, the phone has a triple camera setup with a 50MP sensor. Unlike Pro , Xiaomi 12 has used Sony sensor . The primary sensor has Optical Image Stabilization (OIS) and is accompanied by a 13MP ultrawide and a 5MP telemacro lens.

    Performance and Memory

    Like Xiaomi 12 Pro, This one has Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 chipset . There are three memory configurations including: 8/128GB, 8/256GB, and 12/256GB. All variants use DDR5 RAM and UFS 3.1 storage. The phone is equipped with MIUI 13 based on Android 12 .


    Specifications :

    • CPU : Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 (4nm Mobile Platform)
    • REAR CAMERA : 50 MP primary
    • RAM : 8/12 GB
    • FRONT CAMERA : 32 MP
    • SCREEN SIZE : 6.7 inches
    • STORAGE : 128/256 GB
