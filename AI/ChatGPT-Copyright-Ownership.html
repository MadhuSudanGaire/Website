    Who Owns Content Generated By ChatGPT AI?

    Copyright Ownership related queries are obvious to be raised when it comes to Artificial Intelligence. According to laws,"Copyright is a type of intellectual property law that gives creators of original works exclusive rights to control the use of their creations for a limited period of time. Though the laws might be slightly different as per country but they've the same objective i.e to protect creator's ownership.

    Incase of Artificial Intelligence, Some experts believe that the copyright ownership should be given to the company/organization that develop such AI products. However, other experts belive that this isn't a fair decision to regard them as the owners of such creative creations as this seems to be misleading. Again, the question arises, Who owns content generated by ChatGPT AI?
