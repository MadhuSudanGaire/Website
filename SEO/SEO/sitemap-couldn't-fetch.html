    How to fix sitemap couldn't Fetch Problem in Google Search Console




    What is the primary role of Google Search Console ?

    Google Search Console is a free service offered by Google that helps you monitor, maintain, and troubleshoot your site's presence in Google Search results. You don't have to sign up for Search Console to be included in Google Search results, but Search Console helps you understand and improve how Google sees your site.

    What is sitemap in Google Search Console ?

    A sitemap is a file where you have to provide all the informations about the pages, videos, and other files on your site, and it helps to maintain the relationships between them. The common search engines like Google read this file sothat it could crawl your site more efficiently and effectively to display the most valuable content in the search engine. A sitemap primary duty is to tell the google , which pages and files you think are important in your site, and also provides valuable information about these files. Any new pages and informations in the site is crawled with the help of sitemap sothat every particular page in the site be indexed on google.

    Reason behind Sitemap Couldn't Fetch Error ?

    Sitemap couldn't fetch is a very common error in google search console which is faced by almost every new creator who is new in google search console and SEO related works. There are many reasons of sitemap couldn't fetch error. This error may occur if you submitted your sitemap URL incorrectly. If that's the case then once you need to check to see that the URL path in the sitemap field is correct or not.
    To check whether your sitemap url is correct or not, Follow the steps mentioned below.

    How To Solve Sitemap Couldn't Fetch Problem ?

    • Generate a sitemap.xml file from a website called Sitemap Generator for FREE
    • After Creating a sitemap.xml file , Download it and copy & paste it to the root folder of your website.
    • Now Make sure you have kept the sitemap.xml file named as sitemap.xml only.
    • After doing all these, now go to the Google Search Console / Sitemap interface and submit sitemap.xml file .
    • Now, Even doing all these steps if it shows sitemap couldn't fetch then wait for atleast 2-3 weeks sothat Google Fetch all of the contents in your website.
    • Keep uploading new posts on your website and keep indexing your new pages in google search console.
    • Now , your sitemap will be published without any error with 100% success rate.
