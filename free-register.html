    How to register a .com.np domain for Free in Nepal ?

    The domain .com.np belongs to the Nepal and anyone with Nepalese Citizenship can get a free .com.np, .org.np, .edu.np domain. Most of the bloggers in Nepal use .com.np domain as their primary choice which is 100% free and cn be hosted using Free Hosting platforms as well.

    Easy Steps to register FREE .com.np domain

    1. Visit the official .np Domain Registration site CLICK HERE
    2. Now, you need to search the domain name that you want to keep.
      NOTE : You can't keep domain names different from your government id's like citizenship,passport,etc.
    3. If the name is valid,then it will show "Domain Available"sign.If not try another name.
    4. After that,click on the Register Now button and sign up if you are new on the site or sign in if you've already made an account setup.
    5. To create a new account, you need to fill the form shown below in the picture which includes your name,email,password,etc.
    6. Now,Fill your sitizenship card or any governmental id's and submit it.
    7. After that, wait for the approval from .np registeration team. It usually takes 1,2 days to get approved.
    8. If your informations are correct,then your domain will be active .
