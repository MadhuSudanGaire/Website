    List of Important Policy violations To Avoid


    There are several important policy violations that can prevent a beginner from obtaining AdSense approval for his/her website. Some of the key violations to be mindful of include:

    • Insufficient content: Having a website with thin or inadequate content can hinder AdSense approval. It is crucial to create substantial and valuable content that offers relevant information to your audience.

    • Unstructured or difficult-to-navigate website: A website with poor structure, broken links, or a confusing user interface can negatively impact the user experience and may hinder AdSense approval.

    • Copyrighted content: Using content, images, or media that infringe upon someone else's intellectual property rights can lead to rejection. Ensure that all the content on your website is original or properly attributed to the rightful owner.

    • Adult content or restricted material: Websites that contain adult content, promote violence, or offer restricted products or services may not be eligible for AdSense approval.

    • Deceptive software downloads: Websites that promote or distribute deceptive software, malware, or viruses will not be approved for AdSense. Users' safety and trust are of utmost importance.
