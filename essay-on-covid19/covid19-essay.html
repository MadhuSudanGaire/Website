    Introduction

    Corona Virus which is commonly known as COVID-19 is an infectious disease that causes illness in the respiratory system in humans. Covid 19 was officially declared as Pandemic by World Health Organization(WHO) after its outbreak wholeover the world.The term Covid 19 is sort of an acronym, derived from “Novel Corona Virus Disease 2019”. Corona Virus has affected our day to day life. This pandemic has affected millions of peoples, who either got sick or killed due to the spread of this disease.

    Covid 19 is a virus related to respiratory systems . The virus obstructs the air passage in the body and freezes the lungs and nasal areas. It is spread from person to person among those in close contact within 6 feet. Corona Virus directly attacks the immune system and makes the body weak , because of which the virus is more likely to spread in elderly people and those whose immune system is unhealthy.





    Main Content

    Coronavirus (or COVID-19) was first identified in December 2019 in Wuhan city of China. In March 2020, the World Health Organisation (WHO) declared the Corona Virus outbreak a pandemic. The most common symptoms of this viral infection are fever, cold, cough, bone pain, and respiratory problems. Apart from these symptoms like Fatigue, Sore throat, muscle pain, loss of smell or taste can also be seen in Corona Virus patients. As every virus spread from one person to another,Corona Virus also infects from an infected patient to a healthy body by various means like coughing, sneezing or sharing materials .

    Covid 19 made us realize the importance of cleanliness,health and all the healthy behaviours that we need to apply in our daily lives.Not only this, The virus also gave an important lesson to not hurt any species of animals for our profits. Because of the pandemic,people were caught in their own homes day and night.Noone was allowed to meet with others and had to apply other ways to communicate . Covid 19 not only impacted on health sector but almost in every field. Teaching & learning had to be done online, Industries were closed, Shortage of food took place, Necessity of hospitals & doctors was clearly visible during such harsh situations, People lost their lives,friends,relatives, everyone had to live inside their own home. Because of carelessness of hunanbeing ,Covid 19 had taught a very good lesson to mankind.

    We are well known about the fact that during the pandemic, students had to learn things via online means, business works were done through internet, all the stuffs were bought and sold via online platforms. Though Internet played a crucial role during the harsh pandemic period, but we shouldn't forget our duties as a human and a civilized animal about the rules of nature.





    Conclusion

    In short, Covid 19 was a lesson for mankind which was sent by the nature. Corona Virus was a reminder to humanbeings that the Earth isn't only our home to live but is the shelter for millions of species all around. The loss of lives and the shortage of everything was just a reminder to humans, to show their position infront of mother nature.
