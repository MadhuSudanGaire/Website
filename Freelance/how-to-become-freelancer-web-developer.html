    Freelance Web Developer



    A freelance web developer is the one who builds web based applications independently and can work from home, coffee shops, or libraries – or anywhere around the globe with the availability of internet connection. A freelance web developer delivers his/her skills to the clients remotely even without the physical presence in the client's workshops.

    A freelance web developer is independent and can work with several clients at the same mean time. He/She isn't obligue to any pressure by the clients. The success & failure of a freelancer depends on his works efficiency & talents . If he is able to manage his time and work then he can independently work for anyone. Working as a freelance web developer allows you to face various projects from different employers.

    Typically a freelance developer offers services such as creating software, designing websites, and ensuring a website is mobile devices compatible or not. The handeling of web applications is in the hands of such freelancers & helps to boost the online presence of a company.

    How to become a freelance web developer?

    In order for you to become a freelance web developer, you need to address some of the important key points. Those key points and their proper evaluation is important to understand the major roles of a freelance web developer with every client. The necessary key points are mentioned below:

    • Determine Your Goal: Before all the other necessities, first you need to figure out what kind of services you can offer as a freelance web developer. Web Development may seem easy but to become a full-stack web developer, you need to know what A website is actually composed of.

      Frontend Web Development: Frontend web developers are responsible for ensuring user experience on a web application. Frontend is all about user interface which allows a user to interact with only visual aspects of a web application. The main components of frontend web development is composed of HTML, CSS & Javascript. The HTML works as the structure while CSS works as beautification and Javascript works as logical inputs in a webpage.

      Backend Web Development: It's a part of web development that involves the creation and maintenance of the server, database, and application logic that runs on the server. Backend developers are solely responsible for creating and managing the rib that supports the frontend of the web application. They use various programming languages such as Python, Ruby, Java, and PHP to create the serverside logics and write codes to interact with the database.

      >FullStack Web Development: Both the frontend and backend knowledge can help a freelancer not only to get clients easily but also get higher paid jobs. A company would rather choose person with both capabilites rather than 2 separate individuals. So, Start learning frontend and make backend programming your power . It'll be easier for you to combine the frontend and backend codes which reduces time consumption & help run the system better. In other words, a full-stack developer must have the full capacity to handle everything related to web development.

    • Technicsl Skills: Being a web developer doesn't mean you just need some knowledge on programming languages only. Aside from those programming knowleges, one must have techical knowledge to manage the website. Its a good idea to learn these skills to promote yourself to the next level of web development field. As a freelance web developer, technical skills reflect and make you more competitive & versatile.

      The technical skills in web development includes the following criterias:

      • Responsive design
      • Back-end knowledge
      • Search Engine Optimization(SEO)
      • Testing and debugging
      • Analytical mind.
      • Business skills and so on.

      These skills can help a web developer to gain some height in his post as they increase the chances of getting hired by top clients with less competitors in the market. A freelancer with these skills can achieve most of the things as a freelancing web developer. He/she can start his/her own startups and even teach new freelancers. The scopes get wider with more technical knowledge.

    • Building a portfolio: If you are truely passionate to become a freelance web developer then you must have your past works stored somewhere with you. So, A portfolio is all about featuring your works & talents in the form of presentation. Designing a portfolio creatively can let visitors see a reflection of your web development skills which ultimately increases the potential of getting hired.

      Since the main motive of your portfolio is to attract new clients, make ensure that you are reachable. Put your email address, social media profiles, and contact form sothat they can contact you if they find you a better option. You can create a website, and for that you should have the knowledge about Domain, and web hosting.

      If you are new on web development, you maynot have much knowledge about these things but if you are already a web developer then make sure you choose proper domain address and hosting service to promote your business.

    • Research and Marketing: Being a web developer, you must be able to create websites on every Niche. Besides you need to research the market first. Understand the demand in the market & uplift yourself from small businesses near you. You can create websites to the restraunts, hotels, shops near you and promote yourself locally. This will grow the chances of yours to freelance in an offline environment.

      You can create blog posts of your own and build a bold impression in the web. Working in a trending niche can help you gain more visitors and market yourself. Ranking your website can be challenging but if your content has some quality it shouldn't be an issue. This fosters large number of audience to engage in your posts and help you market yourself.

    How Much Does A Freelance Web Developer Earn?

    Talking about a Fresh beginner freelance webdeveloper, he/she can earn 5-25 US Dollars per project. Whereas a professional freelance web developer's earnings can range from 30K-100K US Dollars + depending on the projects.

    Earning depends upon user's expertise, past experiences and location as well. One can earn higher if he/she gets clients from developing countries. A freelancer gets paid depending upon various approaches. They are:

    • Project-based pricing
    • Time-based pricing

    Project-based pricing: When a freelancer completes a project, he/she gets paid for the work done. Most of the clients prefer this sothat they can trust the abilities of freelancers. On the other hand, this motivates freelancers to work their best for the project as they get paid if the project works fine without any errors. This is beneficial approach for both the client and freelancer.

    Time-based pricing: In this approach, a freelancer gets paid on hourly basis. The more you work for the client, the more you get paid. The income of the freelancer depends on his performance and past work experiences as well. This makes the freelancer busy and the client to direct the work accordingly.

    Perks of Being Freelance Web Developer

    If you are passionate in web development and work independently without any pressure, then Freelance Web Developer can be a good choice. Here are some perks that come along with working as a freelance web developer:

    • Freedom To Work Independently: The major reason to choose freelancing is letting yourself choose your own working category independently. If you feel uncomfortable with the clients and the type of project, you can leave whenever you want. This not only helps you do the works you are interested in but also encourages you to work more for the client from your sub-conscious mind. This helps you get less stress if compared to other corporate jobs, where you can't work in freedom.

    • Flexible working Time: This is another perk of freelancing where you can work anytime you want. You can work irrespective of your location, either while travelling or at home. There's no one who will force you to work on the exact time they want. You don't need to work 9-5 in a fixed place all the time. . Flexible working time helps you to work on several projects at the same time which increases your revenue and helps to understand the importance of time management.

    • Control over workload: Freedom to work independently and flexible working time helps you to focus on your goals and completion of the projects in no time. Freelancing helps you to control over your workload, this helps you develop both physically and mentally for the work. When there's no workload and everything is upon yourself, then the productivity increases. Increase in productivity is directly proportional to check in balance between work and client's relationships.

    • Earning potential: When you become a professional web developer then you can charge more for the work done. Being a freelancer, you can work with many clients and earn higher than any other jobs. Overally, you can work on your own projects and market and sell it. Plenty of opportunities arise once you become a good freelancer. You can start your own youtube channels and social medias and share your ideas with beginners. This helps you as an extra source of income without any kind of mental pressure.

    There are more perks like professional growth, diverse range of experience, location independence and many more which can be some of the reasons to choose freelancing profession as web developer.

    Drawbacks of Being Freelance Web Developer

    Though we talked about the advantages of being a freelance web developer, there are many drawbacks of having such profession. They are listed below:

    • Busy Shedule: Freelancers are fully responsible for finding their own clients and projects all the time, which can be time-consuming and requires a lot of efforts too. This can lead to a busy schedule, as freelancers may have to work on multiple projects simultaneously in order to make a living. Because of these they may get less time to enjoy as they need to complete the project to earn some money.

    • Financially Unstable: As Freelancing isn't a monthly salary basis job, you need to keep working to earn some income. Sometimes you may spend your money on other stuffs and may have no money left. In that case, it is kindof difficult for you to manage the money. In order to start earning, you must complete the projects which may create instability in financial condition of a freelancer.

    • No protection from unemployment: You clearly know that a freelancer may loose his interest anytime if he/she doesn't love his works. If you're not passionate about freelancing then you shouldn't start doing it. One can be feel like unemployed if didn't get any clients ready to work with them. There's no any pansion of being a freelancer. Your success fully depends on your work.

    • No paid vacation or sick leave: If you are travelling person then freelancing can be both boon and curse at the same time. This is because your sub-conscious mind gets distracted whenever you try to enjoy and work. You need to balance both the works at the same time. There's no any leave in freelancing. If you work less, you earn less. But if you think health is your true wealth and rest taking some leave from work then its upon you to check whether its good or bad decision.

    As every coin has two sides, Freelancing is no far betrayed from this truth. If you work in this field wholeheartedly and want to achieve something then this can be a good option. But for those who are doing without interest may live like a rose covered in thrones. By the way, freelancing is both good and bad option to choose.
