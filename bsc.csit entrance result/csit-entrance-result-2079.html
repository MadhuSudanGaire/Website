    BSC.CSIT Entrance Exam is conducted by Tribhuvan University,Institute of Science and Technology (IOST) every year . The entrance exam is conducted for bachelor level education in computer science which is a 4 year course. The entrance exam is conducted wholeover Nepal,and thousands of students give the entrance exam to study further computer science related education. The course helps students to learn some IT related educations where they learn physics,maths,computer science related courses. Students studying this course for 4 years can learn Web Development, Softwaare Development,Data Analytics,and many more.
